Full Body Workout Program

This program is designed for beginners and focuses on training the entire body across three days per week.

Upper Body

Push-Ups

3 sets × 10–12 reps

Works chest, shoulders, and arms.

Dumbbell Rows

3 sets × 10 reps

Strengthens back and posture.

Lower Body

Squats

3 sets × 12 reps

Targets legs and glutes.

Lunges

3 sets × 10 reps each leg

Improves balance and leg strength.

Full Body

Plank

3 sets × 30–45 seconds

Builds core strength.

Jumping Jacks

3 sets × 30 reps

Improves endurance and cardio.
