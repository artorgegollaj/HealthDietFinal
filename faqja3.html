Training Programs

Choose a program that matches your goals. Each one has simple workout ideas and a basic weekly structure for beginners.

Full Body Workout

3 days per week, simple exercises for the whole body.

  • Day 1: Upper body
  • Day 2: Lower body
  • Day 3: Full body

Fat Loss Program

Focus on burning calories with light weights and more movement.

  • 4 days training per week
  • Walking after workouts
  • Simple calorie-control tips

Muscle Gain Program

For people who want to build muscle with heavier weights.

  • Push / Pull / Legs split
  • Progressive overload
  • Focus on protein intake

Home Workout

No gym needed – bodyweight exercises you can do anywhere.

  • Push-ups, squats, lunges
  • Planks and sit-ups
  • Short 20–30 minute sessions
